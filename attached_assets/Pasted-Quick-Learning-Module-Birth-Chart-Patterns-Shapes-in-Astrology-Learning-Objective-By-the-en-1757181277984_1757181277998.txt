🔹 Section 1: What Are Birth Chart Shapes?
In astrology, chart shapes describe the overall distribution of planets in a natal chart. These visual formations reflect how a person focuses energy and experiences the world. Introduced by astrologer Marc Edmund Jones, these 7 patterns are used by astrologers to summarize major life dynamics at a glance.

🔹 Section 3: Hemispheric Emphasis – The 4 Quadrants of Life
